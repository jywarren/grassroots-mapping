Collaborations

GonzoEarth, 1337arts.com, coordination on mailing list

Stewart Long of GonzoEarth, Shannon Dosemagen of the Louisiana Bucket Brigade, and Kris Ansin of the University of Tulane's School of Public Health and Tropical Medicine emerged early as leaders in the project, and their efforts have ensured that map-making has continued for close to three months since our initial efforts.

Blue Seals, Greenpeace, Americorps, Tulane, many individuals...

This dense web of collaborations has formed a backbone of support for the effort, and ensured its regularity and sustainability. While the urgency of the disaster itself surely played a role in forging such a network of mutual support, such strong local participation must be an integral part of any participatory mapping effort which expects to be sustained beyond an initial burst of enthusiasm.


Jeffrey Warren 2010-08-03
